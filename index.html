About the Lab

Too many “future” spaces are gimmicky, profit-driven, or simply not built for us. The Black Women’s Futures Lab is a culturally relevant, liberatory tech space where Black women build AI literacy, digital skills, and entrepreneurial confidence without falling into hustle gimmicks or grind culture.

Here, we claim the tools of today—AI, digital literacy, collective knowledge—and rework them to build truly free futures. We share wisdom, test possibilities, and imagine beyond the limits of now.

The future won’t wait. In this Lab, we set the pace.

Who it’s for

  • Gen X, Millennial, and older Gen Z Black women futurists
  • Multi‑gifted, entrepreneurial‑minded, often neurodivergent, and future‑oriented
  • Women raising future‑ready children, building businesses, or preparing for sovereign futures
  • Committed to generational wealth and communal resilience

What it’s not

  • No get‑rich‑quick gimmicks or hustle‑culture grind
  • No spaces that refuse to center Black women—period
  • No tech idolatry—tools serve people, not the other way around
